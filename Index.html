Our Story

More Than
A Haircut

His Essentials was founded in 2014 on a singular belief — every man deserves precision, care, and an experience that goes beyond the chair. Located in Dar Al-Awadi Tower, Sharq, we've built a reputation for excellence across Kuwait City.

Our barbers speak Arabic & English and specialize in everything from sharp fades to full facial and hair treatments — tailored completely to you.
